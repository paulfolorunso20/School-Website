Admissions

Welcome to Our Admissions Process

At Bright Future International School, we are committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment for all students. Our admissions process is designed to ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Medical records and immunization history
  • Passport-sized photographs

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for admission:

  1. Download and fill out the application form.
  2. Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the school office.
  3. Attend an interview or assessment (if required).
  4. Receive an admission decision within 7 working days.
