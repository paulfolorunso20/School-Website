Empowering Future Leaders

Why Bright Future International School

Parents who enroll their kids in RDIS stand to gain a total child. Learning in an all-inclusive environment that caters to their children's mental and physical health. Training kids to become all-around human beings and in extension better citizens. We are a team of highly qualified, passionate, committed, and experienced practitioners who are well-versed in providing outstanding learning opportunities, care, and support that will bring out children's potentials.

The School:
A warm and welcoming place where children feel loved, cared for, and are comfortable enough to learn.

Our Vision:
To produce academic excellence that will stand out among the few in our society and work at large in the fear of God.

Our Mission:
To provide affordable and quality education that meets the global demand for today's child. Hence, we want children to see:

  • Education as a lifelong process
  • Education as a socializing element
  • Education as an element that will link home to Schools
  • Education as a tool that will make them an active participant rather than passive learners.

Top Available Facilities

  1. Well-equipped classrooms with desks, chairs, whiteboards, and instructional materials.
  2. A library with a wide range of books, periodicals, and digital resources to support research, reading, and learning.
  3. Playgrounds, sports fields, and courts for physical education classes and extracurricular sports activities.
  4. Offices for administrative staff, including the principal's office, guidance counselor's office, registrar's office, and accounting office.
  5. Reliable internet connectivity and computers.
  6. Security systems, including cameras, alarms, and controlled access points, to ensure the safety of students, staff, and visitors.
